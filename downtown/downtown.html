Why downtown Dallas?

Dallas offers plenty of things to see and do, especially in the downtown area. Whatever you're in the mood for, downtown has something for you!

Want to enjoy the panoramic view of the Dallas skyline? Checking out the Reunion Tower GeO Deck is the place you ought to go.

If museums, history, and arts are more your thing, make sure to check out Dallas's own Arts District, or in central downtown, there is the Sixth Floor Museum, the Dallas Holocaust and Human Rights Museum, the Dallas Museum of Arts, and the Old Red Museum, to name a few. Many of them offer interesting exhibits and information about historic moments in Dallas. Don't forget Old East Dallas offers plenty to see and do in this arena as well!

Want to be outdoors or checking out hot spots of tourism? The Dallas World Aquarium, Downtown Dallas Trolley Tours, or Klyde Warren Park might be just what you're looking for! All offer great outdoor activies and relaxation.

As always, if you're looking to be entertained, there are a plethora of theaters to choose from, including the ever-popular Majestic and Wyly theaters.
